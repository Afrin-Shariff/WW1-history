NOT WILLING TO FIGHT

Many people in Britian at the start of theh war did not want to fight and some wanted to have nothing to do with the war. There were quite a few reasons for why not to fight, but if you didn't fight then you would be classed as unworthy of life and a disgrace to society.

WHY?

CONSCIENTIOUS OBJECTERS - some people refused to fight because of their religion

PREFERENCE - some men would rather be in a non-combat role but they still wanted to participate in the war and so they took on roles like trench diggers or ambulance drivers.

MEDICAL REASONS - many people had a medical issue and so they could't fight, examples might be 1 leg or a severe heart disease.

WHAT HAPPENED TO THEM

Before conscription came in, they were named as weak or useless, and some of them were imprisoned, and some of them were forced to do hard labour.
